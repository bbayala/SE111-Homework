REVIEW - Interstellar


Ben Ayala April 22, 2026

Interstellar is a thrilling and shocking movie which describes how astronauts embark on a journey into space in search of a suitable planet in which humanity can live. For the sake of mankind, Cooper, played by Mathew McConaughey, has to abandon his family members, because he is destined to rescue man from an uninhabitable universe. Key details in the movie is what makes it so great. For example, the relationship that arises between Cooper and his daughter Murph proves to be significant.

The main reason why Interstellar is an excellent movie is the realistic aspect in it. Space and special effects are extremely impressive and realistic. In addition to the realism of the movie, the strong emotional background created in terms of family bonds is equally impressive, especially because of time dilations. All actors have great roles, Matthew McConaughey performing particularly well. Overall, I would definitely recommend this movie to others.
