REVIEW - The Wolf of Wall Street


Ben Ayala April 22, 2026

The Wolf of Wall Street is a very interesting film that takes you on a wild ride and explains what happens when someone lives their whole life without ever having any kind of control. The plot is about Leonardo DiCaprio’s character and how he starts a business firm dealing with stocks and befriends people that are just as crazy as him. This movie doesn’t stop and keeps jumping from one crazy scene to another, showing you how everyone just continues to party, making all the wrong decisions.

I also enjoyed how the movie is that had a sense of authenticity. The movie seems to be a non-hyperbolic film due to the story behind it. Additionally, Leonardo DiCaprio improvised on a lot of his scenes. This film was nominated for five Oscars but ended up winning no awards. Overall, I enjoyed this movie and would recommend it to others.
